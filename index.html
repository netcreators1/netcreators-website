Benefits

Temperature Range Locker

Locks your room AC temperature between 22°C to 26°C. Prevents overcooling and saves power. A matchbox-sized device placed on your AC.

How It Works

  • Matchbox-sized device placed on your AC unit.
  • Receives input from the IR AC Remote Control.
  • Locks the temperature range – guest cannot set below 22°C.
  • Real-time display shows setpoint and power savings.

Live Dashboard

  • Current Cost / Hr: At 17°C vs locked.
  • Actual Savings Rate: ₹ /hr saved.
  • Real-Time Power Draw: Across all rooms.
  • Room-wise Monitoring: Each room's setpoint and temperature.

Benefits

  • Locks temperature between 22-26°C.
  • Prevents overcooling – saves power.
  • Lower energy bills.
  • Quick ROI – pays for itself in months.

Veriface

Secure face recognition attendance system for staff. Mark your attendance effortlessly by showing your face to the camera – on your desktop or mobile app. Arrival and departure times are automatically recorded with date and time stamps.

Key Features

  • Face Recognition: Secure and accurate facial recognition for attendance marking.
  • Dual Platform: Works on desktop and mobile app – mark attendance from anywhere.
  • Arrival & Departure: Records both check‑in and check‑out times.
  • Date & Time Stamped: Every entry is logged with precise date and time.
  • Monthly Reports: Pull up attendance reports for all employees with date/time records.
  • Customizable: Can be tailored for your organization's specific needs.

How It Works

  • Install the app on your desktop or mobile device.
  • On arrival, show your face to the camera – it marks you "Present".
  • On departure, show your face again – it records your "Exit" time.
  • All data is stored securely with date and time stamps.
  • Generate monthly attendance reports with a single click.

Benefits

  • No more manual attendance registers or biometric fingerprints.
  • Contactless and hygienic – perfect for post-pandemic workplaces.
  • Eliminate buddy punching and time theft.
  • Easy to use – just show your face!
  • Customizable for any organization.
